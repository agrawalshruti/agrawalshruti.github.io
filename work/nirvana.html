Nirvana

Nirvana is a day spa located in downtown Chicago. The owners of the spa wanted to have a modern and high-end luxury appeal to the spa’s clients. They wanted to separate themselves from the quirky and overzealous spas in the area and distinguish themselves as elegant, calm and ethereal.

I used their guidelines to build a simple yet contemporary black, white, and statement green color palette. I stuck with a bold and rounded san serif for Nirvana’s wordmark and simplified and classical serif to create contrast. However, my distinguishing graphic element for the spa’s brand was the bold white orchid and its eye-catching distinction against its deep black backdrop.
