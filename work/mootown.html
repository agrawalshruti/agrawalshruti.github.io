The idea for Cowboy’s Garden came from a double-draw, place and audience draw, out of a top hat. My design challenge was to build a brand for a “coffee shop for cowboys”. After mind-mapping and sketching, I created moodboards for two brand directions -

  • (one) for a sophisticated and cowboy inspired urban coffee shop - Mootown and
  • (two) for a quirky cafe actually serving cowboys and their families Cowboy’s Garden

In the end, brand elements included, a loyalty card, coffee cup and plate, coffee grind paper bag, coffee takeaway cup, among others. The final logo is the one shown in the very beginning and below are some of the iterations I compared this version against.
